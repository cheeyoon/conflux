About us.

Bringing together top talent, modern technologies, and data to help create better real estate outcomes and experiences.

What we do.

We provide solutions that aim to improve the quality and consistency of common real estate activities: building, buying, selling, investing. We do this by emphasizing the use of technology and data to make customer processes easier, smarter, and faster – and hire top talent to put the pieces together.

Why we do it.

Residential real estate is a massive asset class, and often the most important financial decision for consumers. Yet, the space has been largely un-touched by technology & data, and a fragmented provider landscape drives high variance in outcomes: the average experience is underwhelming. We see an opportunity to change the value chain, ultimately driving meaningful impact to end consumers.

Who we are.

Our team has a unique combination of large-scale real estate expertise and deep experience building great businesses and customer services. We’re excited to use these capabilities to bring an institutional approach to real estate, helping drive superior outcomes and experiences.
